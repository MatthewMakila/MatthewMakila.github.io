Who am I?

My name is Matthew Makila, and I am currently a 4th year student studying computer science and sociology at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). I currently hold a 4.0 cumulative GPA and have research experience at CMU, UCSD, and UMBC!

I have a strong passion for being outside, and you'll often find me hiking, exploring the city, trying new foods, thrifting, and playing a lot of tennis.

Currently, I plan to attend graduate school this fall to earn a PhD in Social Computing. I look to use data science and software design principles to combat social and health inequities. I hope to eventually become the Principal Investigator of my own lab, creating a safe space for marginalized people to study social phenomena affecting marginalized people!

Everyone deserves to live, eat, and be clothed without issue.
